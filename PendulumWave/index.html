Explanation

This is a pendulum wave. Each wave is made up of a series of interconnected points that move at differnet speeds.

The speed, as well as the number of nodes, and the number of waves can be adjusted by the user.

  • Left clicking on a ball in a wave will change the wave to a random colour*
  • Right clicking on a ball in a wave will change the wave to be invisible*
  • To change the number of waves in the system, enter the number into the left form and click the button
  • To change the number of nodes in the system, enter the number into the middle form and click the button
  • To change the speed of the system, enter the number into the right form and click the button
  • To change between 2D and 3D mode, press the Toggle 3D button
