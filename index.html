Ellipse Drawer

Instructions

Move the mouse on the canvas to draw ellipses.

Press w and s to control the colour of ellipses drawn.

Press → (right arrow) to increase the ellipse size and ← (left arrow) to decrease the ellipse size.

Press SPACE to clear the canvas.

Options

Brightness
Saturation
Alpha

Smaller values are more transparent

Pen size
Velocity
Always draw
Draw ellipses when not moving the mouse
